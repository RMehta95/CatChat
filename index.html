About Us
An online mental health platform founded by students, for students.


Our goal is to break the stigma of mental health and provide a convenient outlet for student stress relief. Our volunteers are trained psychologists passionate about helping Northwestern students through tough times.

FAQs:


Is online therapy actually effective?

Yes. Numerous research studies have proven the effective potential of online therapeutic conversation.

So I never have to go into CAPS again?

While CatChat is not a substitute for face-to-face therapy, we hope that it will expose more students to mental health resources. Cultivating relationships with our trained proffessionals online may lead to in-person appointments.

What if all the psychologists online are busy talking to other students?

Students will be placed in the queue where they can see how long the wait is. We don't anticipate the waits being too long, but you may want to watch some videos in the resources section in the meantime.


Please contact us is you have any more questions!
